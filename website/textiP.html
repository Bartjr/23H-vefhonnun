iPhone 13

The iPhone 13 features a Super Retina XDR display, available in two sizes: 6.1 inches for the regular iPhone 13 and 5.4 inches for the iPhone 13 mini. Powered by the A15 Bionic chip, it offers better performance and energy efficiency. The dual-camera system has improvements in low-light photography, computational photography, and video recording. With enhanced battery life, 5G connectivity, and a stylish design featuring a ceramic shield front cover, the iPhone 13 is a powerful and sleek device.

iPhone 13 Pro Max

The iPhone 13 Pro Max boasts a Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, providing higher refresh rates for smoother scrolling and improved responsiveness. It is powered by the A15 Bionic chip and features a triple-camera system, including a LiDAR scanner for enhanced low-light photography and augmented reality applications. With improved battery life and a premium design with a stainless steel frame, the iPhone 13 Pro Max is a flagship device offering top-notch performance and advanced camera capabilities.

iPhone 14

The iPhone 14 comes with 6.1-inch and 6.7-inch display options, improvements to the rear-facing camera, and a virtual emergency connectivity feature. The device aims to establish a connection with emergency services when the user is outside Wi-Fi or cellular network coverage. This feature enhances safety and emergency communication capabilities.

iPhone 14 Pro Max

The Apple iPhone 14 Pro Max features a 6.7" Super Retina XDR LTPO display capable of 2000 nits of brightness, a Triple 48MP rear camera, an Apple A16 Bionic chip, 6GB of RAM, iOS 16.4.1, and new colors such as Space Black and Deep Purple. With advanced features and a stylish design, the iPhone 14 Pro Max is a premium device catering to users who demand cutting-edge technology.

iPhone 15

All iPhone 15 models are fast-charging capable, able to charge up to 50% in 30 to 35 minutes with a 20W or higher power adapter. iPhone 15 models display a number of charging cycle graphs for the first time, and there is also a built-in setting to prevent the iPhone from charging beyond 80% to preserve battery health.

iPhone 15 Pro Max

For the iPhone 15 Pro Max, we designed a 5x zoom camera with the longest optical zoom ever in an iPhone that fits into our compact Pro camera system. Now you can take sharper close-up shots from farther away - like a fantastic picture of your friend or the goal in your child's soccer game.
